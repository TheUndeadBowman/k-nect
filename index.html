Why K-Nect?

K-Nect is useful if:

  • You have a lot of server members and you want to add them to a group DM, but adding them manually would be very time-consuming

  • You want to chat, but Jimmy is annoying you, trying to get into the convo

  • You want to have some fun in your dead server.
  • You want to help Bowman by inviting all his bots (gotta invite 'em all!)
